About Me

Hi,

I'm Ben Malin, and this is my portfolio!

After years of treating tech and the web as a hobby, I finally decided to pursue this passion as a career in 2017. Since I made this switch, I have gained a host of web development skills ranging from jQuery to a full MERN Stack. While this stack incorporates back-end technologies, my passion truly lies in the front-end. I now seek to hone my front-end skills as a junior developer and combine them with my education and professional background to add unique value to a development team.

During my web development education and training, I worked on several team projects to create a variety of specialized web applications. Through this work, I have also improved my abilities
