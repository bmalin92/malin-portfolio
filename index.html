Hi,

I'm Ben Malin, and this is my portfolio!

My love for the web had been with me since I was a child, when I learned how to do basic HTML on forums I frequented. As I grew up, so did technology, and the evolution of tech kept this passion thriving. While it took me a long time to finally realize it, web and mobile technology is my greatest passion, and working as a developer is what I was always meant to do.

Since I started my studies in development, I've learned how to be successful as a full-stack developer, using libraries and frameworks such as React, Redux, React Native, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB, and jQuery. While I lean more toward the front-end of development, I still have the full-stack experience to supplement my work.
