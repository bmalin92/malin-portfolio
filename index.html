About Me

Hi,

I'm Ben Malin, and this is my portfolio!

I am a web developer focused on the MERN Stack. While this stack incorporates back-end technologies, my passion lies in the front-end. When building on the front-end, I love the immediacy of seeing users interact with my apps.

Outside of web development, I watch and critique movies, which is a passion I'll soon be building into a podcast. When I'm not watching the latest critically acclaimed films, I enjoy building computers, tinkering with smart-home technology, and researching the cryptocurrency market.
